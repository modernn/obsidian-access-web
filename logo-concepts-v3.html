Obsidian Access — Logo Concepts V3

Direction: The A IS the shield. O incorporated as element.

Core Concept

01 — A-Shield with O Core

The A forms the protective shield. O sits at the core — the access point.

02 — Open A-Shield + O Ring

Both letters as outlines. Lighter, more refined.

Security Focus

03 — A-Shield + O Keyhole

The O becomes a keyhole within the A-shield. Direct lock/access symbolism.

04 — A-Shield + O Crown

O sits at the apex like a crown or watchtower. Surveilling from above.

05 — O Contains A

O encompasses all. A carved into it as negative space.

Combines All

06 — Slashed A-Shield + O

A as shield with slash (from reference). O positioned alongside.

07 — Monolith A (O implied)

Just the A. The triangle cutout implies the O. Maximum simplicity.

Obsidian

08 — Faceted Obsidian A

The A with obsidian-like facets. Light plays across the surface. Volcanic glass.

09 — A-Frame + O Lock

A as open frame structure, O rendered as a padlock hanging within.

10 — Razor A

Razor sharp. No crossbar, no O. Pure form. The absence is the statement.
